A Taste of Forgotten Flavours

In the quiet heart of Mylapore, Haritam was born from a longing—to bring back the flavours that once filled Tamil homes, when cooking was not rushed but revered.

Our Chef’s Philosophy

We work with native grains, greens and spices—Khapli wheat, millets, cold-pressed oils, freshly ground podis. No preservatives; just clarity of flavour.

The Haritam Way

  • Sourcing: Seasonal produce from local farmers we know by name.
  • Preparation: Stone-ground batters; hand-pounded spice blends.
  • Cooking: Firewood stoves, earthen pots, brass & wood serveware.
  • Serving: Banana leaves, Athangudi tiles, the glow of brass lamps.
